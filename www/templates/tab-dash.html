The ANCHM mission is to provide grassroots advocacy for accident victims and hospital patients by filling the gaps between illness, injury, loss and recovery; through post-­hospitalization care and guidance. Health studies prove the importance of care management strategies to reduce readmissions which increase positive patient outcomes and keep healthcare costs low. ANCHM works directly with medical institutions to ensure patients have adequate post­-discharge assistance.
We help with the following
  • Appointments: Ensure PCP follow-up and/or subsequent appointments.
  • Medication: Support patient's medication regimen and reconciliation.
  • Education: Healthcare literacy for more informed decision making.
  • Connections: Connecting patients to resources for a sustainable recovery.
